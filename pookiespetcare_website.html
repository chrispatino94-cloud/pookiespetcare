Meet Chris

A local pet sitter who
treats pets like family.

Hi, I'm Chris, the person behind Pookie's Pet Care. I started this because I know how much trust it takes to let someone care for your pets and enter your home. My goal is simple: give your pets reliable, personal care while giving you peace of mind.

I provide local dog walking, drop-in visits, and house sitting with one-on-one attention — never group walks. I'm calm and patient with every pet, whether they're a high-energy pup, a shy cat, or a senior who needs a little extra time.

You'll get updates after every visit, and I treat your pets the way I'd want someone to treat mine. If that sounds like what you're looking for, reach out — we'll start with a free meet-and-greet so you can see if it's a good fit.
