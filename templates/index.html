Frequently Asked Questions

A few quick answers before you reach out.

Do you offer a free meet-and-greet?

Yes. I like to meet you and your pet first so we can go over the routine, personality, house access, and make sure it feels like a good fit before the first visit.

What areas do you serve?

I serve the South Denver Metro area, including Lone Tree, Parker, Castle Rock, Highlands Ranch, Aurora, Bennett, and nearby areas depending on timing and availability.

Do you send updates?

Yes. I send a quick visit recap and photo update so you know how your pet did.

Do you care for cats too?

Yes. I offer drop-in visits for cats, including feeding, water, litter checks, playtime, and updates.

Do you do overnight care?

Yes. Overnight care is available for pets who do better staying in their own home and keeping their normal routine.

Do you take last-minute bookings?

Sometimes. It depends on my schedule, location, and the type of care needed. Text me and I'll let you know if I can help.

Do you work holidays and weekends?

Yes, holidays and weekends are available. Major holidays may have holiday pricing.

How do I book?

Text Chris to start. We'll do a free meet-and-greet first, then confirm the service, schedule, and care details.
